About caption

A caption is a brief explanation or description accompanying an illustration, photograph,diagram, or similar item, typically to provide context or additional information.Captions can help viewers understand the content better or emphasize specific points within the visual medium.In the context of social media, captions are often used to complement images or videos,providing context, humor, or additional details.
